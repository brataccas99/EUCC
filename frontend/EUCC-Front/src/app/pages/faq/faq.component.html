Frequently Asked Questions

The EU Contracts Requirements Checker is a tool that uses Llama2 to check if a given contract complies with the requirements of the European Union.

To use the EU Contracts Requirements Checker, simply upload your contract to the tool and it will check if it complies with the requirements of the European Union.

The requirements of the European Union for contracts vary depending on the type of contract and the parties involved. The EU Contracts Requirements Checker checks for compliance with all relevant EU regulations and directives.
