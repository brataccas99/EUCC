About EU Contract Requirements Checker

Our project aims to help businesses from non-EU countries enter the EU market by providing a tool to check their compliance with EU contract requirements.

Our Mission

We believe that every business should have the opportunity to enter the EU market, regardless of their location. Our mission is to provide a simple and effective tool to help businesses from non-EU countries comply with EU contract requirements.

Our Vision

We envision a world where businesses from all over the globe can easily enter the EU market and compete on a level playing field. Our tool will help to reduce barriers to entry and promote fair competition.
